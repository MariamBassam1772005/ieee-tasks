Bougainvillea

Infernal flowers shine with their bright pink color, like silk cloths planted under the warm sun. Among its bright leaves, its small white flowers hide to add a touch of softness and tranquility to the vibrant landscape. It is a living embodiment of summer beauty,always reminding us of the need to celebrate bold colors and bright moments in life.

Rose

This rose blooms in warm colors that blend bright white and red, decorated with shiny dew drops. A hardworking bee is approaching its nectar, in a natural scene that embodies the harmony between the beauty of flowers and the work of living organisms under the light sun.

Calendula

The calendal flower glows with its orange and warm yellow color,like a small sun shining between the dark green leaves.The petals pulsate with absolute vitality, centered by a dark ring of reddish-brown,which adds depth to their radiant beauty. It is the embodiment of energy and radiance, and its cheerful mood illuminates wherever it is grown.
